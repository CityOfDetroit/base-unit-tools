# What is a building?

Buildings are fixed structures with a roof and walls. The City monitors the processes of constructing of new buildings, rehabilitating buildings, and the demolition of damaged buildings.

# What are the important attributes of a building?

- Link to the parcel of land that the building is located on
- Link to any units that represent subdivisions of the building (like an apartment)
- Addressed vs Unaddressed: Some buildings like garages don't have their own address
- Building Status: (pending, active, retired)
- Physical characteristics: number of units, height, condition
